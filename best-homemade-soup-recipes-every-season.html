How to Make the Best Homemade Soup: 8 Recipes for Every Season

📅 2026-05-25 ⏱️ 2 min read 📝 564 words

Homemade soup is comfort in a bowl. It's also one of the cheapest, easiest, and most forgiving things to cook. You can't really mess up soup — too thick? Add broth. Too thin? Simmer longer. These 8 recipes cover every craving.

1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

Sauté diced onion, carrots, and celery in butter for 5 min. Add 8 cups chicken broth, 1 lb diced chicken breast, thyme, salt, pepper. Simmer 15 min. Add 2 cups egg noodles, cook 8 more minutes. The cure for everything from colds to bad days. Feeds 6 for about $8.

2. Creamy Tomato Soup

Sauté diced onion and garlic in butter. Add 2 cans (28 oz) crushed tomatoes, 2 cups chicken broth, 1 tsp sugar, basil, salt, pepper. Simmer 20 min. Blend until smooth (immersion blender or regular blender). Stir in ½ cup heavy cream. Serve with grilled cheese. Restaurant quality for $5.

3. Loaded Baked Potato Soup

Cook 5 large diced potatoes in 6 cups chicken broth until tender (15 min). Mash some potatoes against the side of the pot for thickness. Add 1 cup heavy cream, 1 cup shredded cheddar, salt, pepper. Top each bowl with bacon, chives, sour cream, and more cheese.

4. Beef Stew

Brown 2 lbs beef stew meat in batches. Add diced potatoes, carrots, onion, 4 cups beef broth, 2 tbsp tomato paste, thyme, bay leaf. Simmer 1.5-2 hours until beef is fork-tender. Thick, hearty, and feeds a crowd. Even better the next day.

5. Chicken Tortilla Soup

Sauté onion and garlic. Add 6 cups chicken broth, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 can black beans, 1 can corn, cumin, chili powder, and 1 lb chicken breast. Simmer 20 min. Shred chicken. Serve with tortilla chips, avocado, lime, and sour cream.

6. Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Sauté onion in butter. Add 4 cups chicken broth and 4 cups chopped broccoli. Simmer until tender (10 min). Make a roux: 3 tbsp butter + 3 tbsp flour, cook 1 min, add 2 cups milk. Combine with soup. Stir in 2 cups shredded cheddar until melted. Creamy, cheesy perfection.

7. Minestrone

Sauté onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Add 6 cups vegetable broth, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 can kidney beans, 1 cup pasta (ditalini or elbow), zucchini, green beans, Italian seasoning. Simmer 15 min. Top with parmesan. A hearty vegetarian soup packed with vegetables.

8. Chili

Brown 1.5 lbs ground beef with diced onion. Add 2 cans kidney beans, 1 can black beans, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 can tomato sauce, 3 tbsp chili powder, cumin, garlic, salt. Simmer 30-60 min (longer = better). Serve with cornbread, shredded cheese, and sour cream. Feeds 8 for about $12.

Soup Tips

  • Always sauté aromatics first (onion, garlic, celery, carrots in butter/oil). This builds flavor.
  • Season at the end. Salt intensifies as liquid reduces. Taste and adjust before serving.
  • Soup freezes beautifully. Make a double batch and freeze in portion-sized containers for instant meals.
  • Serve with bread. Crusty bread, biscuits, or cornbread transforms soup from a side to a full meal.
🎯 Key Takeaway: Homemade soup is cheap ($5-12 feeds 6-8 people), easy (most soups are dump-and-simmer), and comforting. Start with chicken noodle or creamy tomato — they're foolproof. Make double batches and freeze portions for lazy-day meals. Every soup follows the same formula: sauté aromatics, add liquid and ingredients, simmer, and season. Once you understand that pattern, you can improvise endlessly.
